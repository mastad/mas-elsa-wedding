How It All Began

Our Story

Some love stories begin with a glance across a crowded room. Ours began with a smile that neither of us could quite explain — warm, familiar, and full of quiet promise.

Abrham and Blen crossed paths in the kind of unexpected way that only makes sense in hindsight. What started as a friendship built on laughter, long conversations, and shared faith quickly blossomed into something neither of them saw coming — but both of them knew was right.

Through every season, every challenge, and every quiet moment in between, they chose each other. And on August 2nd, 2026, in front of the people they love most, they'll choose each other one more time — for always.

Everything You Need to Know

Questions & Answers

What is the dress code? ▾
We kindly ask all guests to dress in white or silver attire — formal or semi-formal. Please avoid bright colors and any shade other than white or silver. Let's keep the look elegant and cohesive as we celebrate together!
Are children allowed? ▾
Children are welcome! However, parents and guardians are fully responsible for supervising and managing their children throughout the event. We kindly ask that you keep them close and ensure they do not disrupt the ceremony or reception. Thank you for your cooperation!
Where is the venue located? ▾
The wedding will be held at Beza Event Center, located at 1815 Woodland Ave, Columbus, Ohio 43219. Complimentary parking is available on site.
Can I take photos during the ceremony? ▾
We are having an unplugged ceremony — we kindly ask that phones and cameras be put away so everyone can be fully present. Our photographer will beautifully capture every moment. You're absolutely welcome to take photos during the reception!
When should I RSVP by? ▾
Please RSVP by July 30, 2026 so we can finalize our seating and catering arrangements. We appreciate your timely response!
Is there parking at the venue? ▾
Yes! Complimentary parking is available at Beza Event Center. We encourage carpooling or arranging a ride share if you plan to enjoy the evening fully.
What time should I arrive? ▾
The ceremony begins promptly at 5:00 PM. We recommend arriving 15–20 minutes early to find your seat and get settled before the celebration begins.
