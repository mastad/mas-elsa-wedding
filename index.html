How It All Began

Our Story

Ours isn't a love story that began with a glance across a room — it began years earlier, in the small town of Wolliso, Ethiopia, where two children shared books, laughter, and a childhood neither of them ever forgot.

Life pulled them apart — different universities, different cities, different continents — until a Facebook "people you may know" suggestion brought them back together. A simple "endet neh?" turned into long phone calls, coffee dates in Silver Spring, and the realization that some bonds only grow stronger with distance and time.

On a quiet evening in Alexandria, Virginia, Mas got down on one knee and told Elsa she was his first love, his answered prayer, his greatest blessing. She said yes. 💍 Now, on September 19th, 2026, in Washington, DC — what started in Wolliso becomes forever.

Everything You Need to Know

Questions & Answers

What is the dress code? ▾
We kindly ask all guests to dress in white or silver attire — formal or semi-formal. Please avoid bright colors. Let's keep the look elegant and cohesive!
Are children allowed? ▾
Children are welcome! However, parents are fully responsible for supervising their children throughout the event.
Where is the venue? ▾
Mt Horeb Baptist Church, 2914 Bladensburg Rd NE, Washington, DC 20018.
Where should I park? ▾
Parking at the venue itself is limited, so please park at Debre Meheret Kedus Michael Ethiopian Orthodox Cathedral, 3010 Earl Pl NE, Washington, DC 20018, directly in front of the venue — this is the main parking spot for guests.
Can I take photos during the ceremony? ▾
We are having an unplugged ceremony — please put phones away. You're welcome to take photos during the reception!
When should I RSVP by? ▾
Please RSVP by August 22, 2026. We appreciate your timely response!
What time should I arrive? ▾
The ceremony begins at 1:00 PM. We recommend arriving 15–20 minutes early.
