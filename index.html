How It All Began

Our Story

Some love stories begin with a glance across a crowded room. Ours began with a smile that neither of us could quite explain — warm, familiar, and full of quiet promise.

Abrham and Blen crossed paths in the kind of unexpected way that only makes sense in hindsight. What started as a friendship built on laughter, long conversations, and shared faith quickly blossomed into something neither of them saw coming — but both of them knew was right.

Through every season, every challenge, and every quiet moment in between, they chose each other. And on August 2nd, 2026, in front of the people they love most, they'll choose each other one more time — for always.

Everything You Need to Know

Questions & Answers

What is the dress code? ▾
We kindly ask all guests to dress in white or silver attire — formal or semi-formal. Please avoid bright colors. Let's keep the look elegant and cohesive!
Are children allowed? ▾
Children are welcome! However, parents are fully responsible for supervising their children throughout the event.
Where is the venue? ▾
Beza Event Center, 1815 Woodland Ave, Columbus, Ohio 43219. Complimentary parking available.
Can I take photos during the ceremony? ▾
We are having an unplugged ceremony — please put phones away. You're welcome to take photos during the reception!
When should I RSVP by? ▾
Please RSVP by July 30, 2026. We appreciate your timely response!
What time should I arrive? ▾
The ceremony begins at 5:00 PM. We recommend arriving 15–20 minutes early.
